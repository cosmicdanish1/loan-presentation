Loan Lifecycle Management

End-to-end automation from application to final disbursement.

STEP 01

Application

Intelligent eligibility engine checks requested amount against Basic Pay, Max Limits, and existing liabilities automatically.

STEP 02

Sanctioning

Configurable multi-level approval workflow. Supports Committee approval and digital signature integration.

STEP 03

Disbursement

One-click disbursement triggers auto-voucher generation, ledger posting, and instant SMS notification to the member.

STEP 04

Top-up Logic

Smart handling of "Loan on Loan" scenarios. System auto-calculates closure of previous loans from new principal.
